                5 Why Framework Plain-English Why questions (Groq & Tavily)

                Requires Groq and Tavily API keys (header → API keys). Ask WHY 1 through WHY 5 one at a time. Each step writes a short, plain-English question about this roadmap’s reason, clarity, and essence using saved fields only — no jargon, no answers, and no assumptions. WHY 2 focuses on priority using MoSCoW, RICE, and KANO data when those sections are filled in. WHY 3 builds on WHY 2, and so on until you reach the deepest question.
